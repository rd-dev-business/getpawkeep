PawKeep Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

PawKeep ("we", "our", "the app") is a pet health tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data PawKeep collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

PawKeep stores all data locally on your device. We do not operate servers or databases that store your personal information. The data you enter includes:

  • Pet information (name, breed, date of birth, weight, photos)
  • Health records (medications, vaccinations, vet visits, symptoms)
  • Expense records (amounts, categories, receipts)
  • Favorite places and locations
  • Documents and uploaded images

Location Data

PawKeep requests location access only for walk tracking and nearby place discovery. Location data is stored locally on your device as part of walk routes. We do not transmit location data to any server.

Camera & Photos

PawKeep requests camera and photo library access to allow you to add pet photos, scan documents, and attach receipt images. Photos are stored locally on your device. Photos taken within the app are saved to your Camera Roll.

HealthKit

PawKeep can record dog walks as workouts in Apple Health and read step count and distance data. This data stays within Apple's HealthKit framework and is not transmitted to any server.

iCloud

PawKeep may use iCloud Drive to store backup copies of your data for recovery purposes. This data is protected by your Apple ID and iCloud security. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

PawKeep may use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — for managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Apple. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry — for crash reporting (anonymous crash data only, no personal information). See Sentry's Privacy Policy.
  • PostHog — for anonymous usage analytics (no personal data collected). See PostHog's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party. Your pet's health information stays on your device.

Data Retention

Your data is stored on your device for as long as you use the app. Deleting the app removes all local data. iCloud backups persist until you delete them from iCloud.

Children's Privacy

PawKeep does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and does not contain age-restricted content.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete any data within the app, or delete the app entirely to remove all data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
